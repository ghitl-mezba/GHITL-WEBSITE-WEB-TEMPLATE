Welcome

Welcome to GHITL

Golden Harvest InfoTech Ltd (GHIT) is a multidisciplinary outsourcing service provider company established in 2000. Golden Harvest InfoTech have been loyally serving special and priority clientele in USA, UK, France, Germany and more.

Our Mission

Our mission is to remain a world-class data processing, diversified and socially responsible company that provides high-quality, value-added products and services for our clients. Our IT services range from data entry to data validation and back office work such as business process outsourcing (BPO), image verification and assessment. Our software team provides full support to the data entry, data processing and data analysis system to develop customized data entry software and quality checking tools for each client project.

Our Vision

Our vision is to deliver 99.9% accuracy while delivering every project on time and within the budget of the project. Our goal is to support the client with any and all outsource back office technology tasks and to accurately and effectively complete all projects. We envision a client relationship that is based on trust and confidence and mutual success.
